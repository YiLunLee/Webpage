About

I'm a 1-st year PhD student in the Enriched Vision Applications Lab at National Chiao Tung University, recently received M.S. in Computer Science from National Chiao Tung University. I'm now advised by Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu and Dr. Yi-Hsuan Tsai.

During my graduate studies, I worked on unsupervised face recognition and image compression under Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu and Dr. Yi-Hsuan Tsai.

My research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
